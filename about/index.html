FAQs

Do you seal areas damaged by wildlife?

Yes – we pride ourselves at keeping the nuisance out and out for good. We even offer warranties!

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes! We are licensed & certified by the DNR, Department of Agriculture and the State of Georgia.

Do you offer a fast response to inquiries?

Yes, we aim to schedule our first visit to your property on the same day as your initial inquiry, if not, this will be arranged at least within 24 hours.

Do you have a callout charge?

Yes, We do have an inspection fee. However, it gets credited towards the overall service.

What are your hours?

We are a 24/6 company (closed on Sundays). Our employees work-life balance is important to us so, a day off is mandatory.

Who is Asap?

We are a family-run business based in Atlanta, Ga, operating in the Metro Atlanta area & outside the perimeter. We pride ourselves on our peace making as well as our accountability.

At ASAP Wildlife Removal, we provide a discreet and thorough service. We treat each property as if it were our own, leaving areas clean and as safe as possible.
